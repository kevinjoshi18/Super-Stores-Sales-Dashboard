# POWER BI PROJECT 1-->

PROJECT OBJECTIVE :

==> To contribute to the success of a business by utilizing data analysis techniques, specifically focusing on time series analysis, to provide valuable insights and accurate sales forecasting.

DESRIPTION :

(1) Dashboard Creation : 

==> Identify the KPI's, design an intuitive and visually appealing dashboard, add interactive visualizations and filtering capabilities to allow users to explore data at various levels of granularity.

(2) Data Analysis :

==> Provide valuable insights to business entities regarding the effectiveness of their sales strategies through visualization and charts.

(3) Sales Forecasting :

==> Leverage historic data and apply time series analysis to generate sales forecasts for next 15 days.

(4) Actionable Insights and Recommendations :

==> End goal is to share valuable insights and actionable information that can drive strategic decision-making and support the supermarket's goals for growth, efficiency, and customer's satisfaction.

PROCESS :

==> The first step is Data Overview.
==> Second step is data cleaning. Here we Transform the Data in the Power Query editor.

   --> Transform - View Data type.
   --> View - Column Quality.(It is used to check the null Quantities or values.)
   --> To remove duplicates - Home --> Remove rows --> Remove duplicates.
   --> Dax Queries are written in data view option.
   --> To create a relationship with tables, model view is used.

==> Finally we have created the Dashboard.

CONCLUSION :

==> Incorporated data analysis techniques, specializing in time series analysis, to deliver valuable insights, accurate sales forecasting, and interactive sales dashboard creation, driving the business success.
